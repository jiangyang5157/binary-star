- **Absorption vs. Rejection**: Macro structure supersedes micro-delta. - **Volume Rule Context**: The `Volume Breakout Ratio >= 2.0` requirement applies ONLY to breakout/momentum trades. Mean-reversion setups from structural extremes (VAH/VAL/POC) EXPECT low volume exhaustion and MUST NOT be blocked by low Breakout Ratios.

- **Breakout Efficiency**: If `Volume Breakout Ratio` >= 2.0 and Price crosses `VAH` (Bull) or `VAL` (Bear), target the next **High Volume Node (HVN)**.
- **Deep Limit Entry (DLE) (Reference Only)**: For review purposes, identify the "optimal" deep entry point. 
    - `LONG_DLE = VAL - (0.5 * ATR)` or `Price at nearest Micro-HVN below Current Price`.
    - `SHORT_DLE = VAH + (0.5 * ATR)` or `Price at nearest Micro-HVN above Current Price`.
    - This is NOT an action, but a structural anchor for performance evaluation.

Your core analysis philosophy relies on:
- **Volume Profile** (POC, VAH, VAL) - Structural Gravity.
